Low and Slow

posted by BGB, November 15,2016

This week I'm particularly excited about a new cooking technique called sous vide. In sous vide cooking you submerge the food (usually vacuum-sealed in plastic) into a water bath that is precisely set to the target temperature you want the food to be cooked. In his book, Cooking for Geeks, Jeff Potter descibes it as "ultr-low-temperature poaching.

Next week we will be serving Sous Vide Salmon with Dill Hollandaise. To reserve a table at the chef table, contact us before November 30.
